Arthas Menethil

My name is Arthas Menethil, also known as "the first of The Lich King's death knights" and later as "The Lich King". I was born in Lordaeron, Eastern Kingdoms, though I now live in Icecrown, Northrend.

I have a wide range of skills when it comes to ruling over people and other things. When I was young I was the Crown Prince of Lordaeron and also a knight in the Order of the knights of the Silver Hand. I was trained as a paladin by the great Uther the Lightbringer.

In my later years took up the cursed runeblade Frostmourne and from there on I took employment as the first death knight to the Lich King. In that employment I had responsibility over Northrend and the Scourge and was seated on top of the Frozen Throne in Icecrown for the years to come. After some time I was promoted to Lich King and my ambition was to take over the whole world of Azeroth.

In my efforts of doing so I had my land invaded by some champions that later killed me and released my spirit. Therefore I now look for a new kind of employment where there are a little to no amount of physical interaction with matter, since I'm in my sprit form.

Accomplishments

  • Killed the necromancer Kel'Thuzad
  • Purging the city of Stratholme
  • Defeated the demon Mal'Ganis
  • Delivered the killing blow to Uther the Lightbringer
  • Enslaved the soul of Sylvanas Windrunner
  • Fought of Illidan Stormrage in single combat
  • Employed (raised) the dragon Sindragosa

Defeats

  • I failed to stop the Scourge, so I joined them
  • I had my land invaded by the champions of Azeroth
  • I failed to conquer the world
  • I was slain in battle
